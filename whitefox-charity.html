About Us

We Believe in the Power of Community

White Fox Charity was founded on a simple belief: that small acts of generosity, when united, can transform entire communities. We work directly with families and local leaders to address root causes of poverty.

Our model is transparent, efficient, and impact-driven. Every naira raised goes toward programs with measurable outcomes — because you deserve to see your donation at work.
