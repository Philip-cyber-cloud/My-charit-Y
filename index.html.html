About Us

Building Stronger American Communities

White Fox Charity is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization headquartered in New York, NY. We work in partnership with local leaders and volunteers across America to tackle poverty, hunger, and lack of educational opportunity.

Since our founding, we've served families in over 20 states, bringing measurable, lasting change to the communities that need it most.

  • Education & Scholarship Programs
  • Food Relief & Hunger Outreach
  • Community Development Initiatives
  • Youth Mentorship Programs

What We Do

Our Programs

Every initiative is designed to create lasting impact in underserved American communities.

📚

Education Support

Scholarships, school supplies, tutoring, and after-school programs for students in low-income families across the U.S.

🍽️

Food Relief

Partnering with local food banks and shelters to deliver nutritious meals to families facing food insecurity nationwide.

🏘️

Community Development

Infrastructure improvements, job-skills training, and neighborhood revitalization projects across underserved U.S. cities.
